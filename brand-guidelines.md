## Typography

### Primary Font
Serif typeface for wordmark and headings — conveys craft and design authority.

### Secondary Font
Satoshi (sans-serif) for body text, UI, and documentation — clean, modern, readable.

### Monospace
JetBrains Mono for code blocks and CLI output.

## Industry
Developer tools, design systems, CLI tooling

## Description
nib is the design control plane — a single orchestration layer that sits between your brand, your design tools, your codebase, and your AI agents. It provides two connected pipelines: brand system generation (guidelines to tokens to platform outputs) and interactive prototype export (Pencil.dev to HTML).
